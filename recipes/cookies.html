Cookies Recipe

Description

These warm, freshly baked cookies are soft in the center with delightfully crisp edges. Packed with rich chocolate chips and a hint of vanilla, they deliver the perfect balance of sweetness and texture. Whether you’re pairing them with a cold glass of milk or sneaking one warm off the tray, they’re impossible to resist.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Baking Soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate chips
  • Nuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture.
  3. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
  4. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake until the edges are golden brown.
